Neighbours & Noise.

Improving student relations with the Sheffield community.

CLIENT: THE UNIVERSITY OF SHEFFIELD.

Convincing students to keep the noise down late at night isn’t the easiest task. I designed and illustrated an animated scene showing a particularly boisterous house and then the exhausted neighbours they were keeping up, encouraging students to encourage the consequences to their actions.

The house and all the characters have since been used to pass on more messages: don’t forget to lock up after you leave the house, make sure you take the bins out and please don’t leave litter on the streets. Its more humorous tone helps what are quite patronising messages sound somewhat less preachy.

The animation was provided by my coworker Kieran Bentley. We created large-size horizontal and vertical formats for use on digital screens across campus, as well as smaller formats for social media.

Thanks for taking a look at this project!

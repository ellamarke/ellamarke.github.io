NUNS Marketing Materials.

Marketing materials for NUNS, a new play about nuns and bad behaviour.

I was approached to create the posters, programmes and further print and digital marketing materials for NUNS, an exciting new production with a female-led cast and production team. Photographer Elise Dumontet provided me with beautiful photos of the cast which I edited to create a coloured neon glow, reflecting the artificial light of the neon illustrations that surround them. I created many illustrations of neon lights, only two of which were used in the final piece.

Thanks for taking a look at this project!

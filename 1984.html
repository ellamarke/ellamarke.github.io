1984 Production Posters.

A poster series for an on-stage production of George Orwell's 1984.

CLIENT: THEATRE GROUP LEEDS.

A theatre group in Leeds approached me with their concept for a modernised imagining of Orwell's 1984, which took advantage of our contemporary setting of omnipresent tech. I decided to play on their use of technology in the production by using the familiar blue crash screen to recreate the ominous and familiar words of Big Brother.

Thanks for taking a look at this project!

So, here I am.

I'm more than just a pretty portfolio.

LET'S DO SOMETHING GREAT TOGETHER one
Through a strong conviction that design is a powerful tool, I believe that all projects can have a positive impact, no matter how small. Whether it's marketing design which encourages a new audience to get excited by theatre, video production which confronts racism in higher education or discovering radical new ways to tackle food poverty, many of my projects question how design can make a difference.
LET'S DO SOMETHING GREAT TOGETHER two
I use a rich, research-led process. It's important to me that all the work I produce doesn't just look great, but comes from a smart concept led by loads of contextual research. If you want to see case studies of how I've applied research to my work in the past,  find examples here.

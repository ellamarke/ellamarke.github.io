University of Sheffield GIF Suites.

Suites of GIFs to get students more involved in their university.

CLIENT: THE UNIVERSITY OF SHEFFIELD.

I designed and illustrated several suites of animated GIFs for The University of Sheffield to help shape a more modern and engaging approach to their student communications.

Reflecting different aspects of university life, each new set has received huge engagement from the student body, gaining a total of 14.1 million total views across social media platforms. The suite was animated by Kieran Bentley.

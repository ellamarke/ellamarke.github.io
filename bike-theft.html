Anti-Bike Theft

A print and digital campaign warning students against bike theft.

CLIENT: THE UNIVERSITY OF SHEFFIELD.

My task here was to convert what is essentially a boring message — to remember to keep your bike locked up and safe — into something that the audience would pay attention to.

I designed a flashing neon-led image demonstrating how an unlocked bike may look to a potential thief. The artwork was later animated by my coworker Kieran Bentley and displayed across social media and live screens across campus.

Thanks for taking a look at this project!

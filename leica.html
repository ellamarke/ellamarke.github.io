Celebrating Leica.

A poster series exploring the technical beauty behind iconic photos.

Many of the world’s favourite photographs were taken with Leica cameras. It seems to be the brand’s commitment to both technical excellence and creativity which has allowed its products to become the vehicle to create such iconic works of art.

The poster series explores this combination of art and technical precision with a technical review of the compositions and shapes found within some of street photography’s most important photographs.
